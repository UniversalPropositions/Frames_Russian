Predicate: плодиться

Roleset id: плодиться.01, multiply, increase Source: multiply.01 (multiply.01)

Roles:

        A0: causer of increase, agent
        A1: thing increasing or multiplier
        A2: amount increased by, EXT or multiplicand
        A3: start point
        A4: end point or product
